Features

Everything your institution needs

A complete platform purpose-built for managing every aspect of academic institutions.

Student Management
Full lifecycle from enrollment to graduation. Track records, documents, fees, and academic progress.
Faculty Portal
Manage assignments, workload, and profiles. Grade, mark attendance, and set assignments with ease.
Course Management
Create courses, sections, and schedules. Handle enrollment caps and prerequisites seamlessly.
Grades & Assessments
Grade entry, GPA calculation, report cards, and transcripts with support for custom grading scales.
Attendance Tracking
Daily attendance with leave applications, automated alerts, and detailed historical reports.
Reports & Analytics
Dashboards with charts, exportable reports, and audit logs for data-driven institutional decisions.
Roles

Built for everyone in your institution

Different roles, tailored experiences. Everyone gets exactly the tools they need.

Administrator
Full platform control — manage all users, departments, data, audit logs, announcements, and settings.
  • Manage students & faculty
  • Department & course oversight
  • Financial & scholarship records
  • Audit logs & data exports
Faculty
Manage your courses, mark attendance, enter grades, post assignments, and communicate with students.
  • Course & section management
  • Grade entry & assessment
  • Attendance marking
  • Assignment management
Student
View enrollment, grades, attendance, assignments, and generate transcripts and hall tickets.
  • Course enrollment & wishlist
  • Grade & GPA calculator
  • Attendance & leave tracking
  • Transcript & hall ticket export
